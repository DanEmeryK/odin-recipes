Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe

About the Dish

Buffalo chicken dip is the superstar of appetizers. It's warm, flavorful, and even has a little bit of a kick to get ya goin'. This recipe is a slightly modified version of the recipe you'll find on a bottle of Frank's Red Hot Buffalo sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole rotisserie chicken
  • 2 cups fiesta blend shredded cheese
  • 2 cups Frank's Red Hot Buffalo
  • 1.5 cup low fat ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup scallion, diced
  • 8 oz low fat cream cheese

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Shred rotisserie chicken, using both white and dark meat.
  3. Microwave cream cheese for 30 seconds, add to large mixing bowl.
  4. Add chicken, Frank's Red Hot Buffalo, ranch dressing, and 1 cup shredded cheese to bowl. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Transfer contents of bowl into oven safe dish. Bake for 20 minutes
  6. At halfway point, add 1 cup of shredded cheese and scallions to top of dish.
  7. Let stand for 10 minutes, enjoy!
